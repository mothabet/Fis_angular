As the Article (19) law from the Center for Statistic and Informationfor NCSI to achieve the goals, The center have the right to request any necessary data, information and administrative records from individuals, public or private sector free of charge. The individuals and entities should provide data without disruption to the rules imposed by the law to ensure the confidentiality and privacy of individual's data.
